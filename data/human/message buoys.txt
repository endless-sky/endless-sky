# Entering Republic space
mission "Message Buoy: Entering Republic space from Pirates"
	entering
	repeat
	destination "Earth"
	invisible
	to offer
		has "chosen sides"
		not "temporary ceasefire"
		not "entered system by: takeoff"
	source
		government "Republic"
		neighbor government "Pirate"
	on offer
		message
			phrase "entering republic space from pirates"
		fail

phrase "entering republic space from pirates"
	word
		"Message buoy to all ships: You have reentered Republic-controlled space"
	word
		". Prepare to be scanned."
		". All ships flying rebellious colors are to comply with Navy personnel or will be deemed valid targets."
		". A reminder that a ship flown by an unlicensed pilot is considered piracy and will be deemed a valid target under the Interstellar Piracy Act."
		". Upon landing in a Republic port, please subject your ship to scanning for any invasive species or pathogens."
		". Are you a Republic Galactic Entry accredited citizen? Skip the queues at Immigration upon presenting your Republic ID."
		" from a system not under Republic jurisdiction. Be forewarned that you may be subject to further questioning upon landing at a Republic port under the Foreign Systems (Relations) Act."
