# Entering Republic space
mission "Message Buoy: Entering Republic space from Pirates"
	entering
	repeat
	destination "Earth"
	invisible
	to offer
		has "chosen sides"
		not "temporary ceasefire"
	source
		government "Republic"
		neighbor government "Free Worlds"
	on offer
		message
			phrase "entering republic space from pirates"

phrase "entering republic space from pirates"
	word
		"Message Buoy: You have reentered Republic-controlled space"
	word
		". Prepare to be scanned."
		". All ships flying rebellious colors are to comply with Navy personnel or will be deemed valid targets."
		". Piracy is a crime against humanity."
