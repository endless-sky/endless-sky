	minor
	job
	name "Alexandria Library Seeker"
	description `Alexandria Library is looking for an Apprentice Seeker. Meet Daemos Titan at the Al-Qarawiyyin Library on Earth to help him seek the Chal Jub Paqmey. Payments will total 400,000 credits. Escorts will be provided through dangerous areas.`

		not planet Earth
		near Sol 7
	destination Earth
	to offer
		not "library card"
		"net worth" > 300000
		has "event: remembrance day"
		or
			not "chosen sides"
			has "main plot completed"
	on complete
		conversation
			`You arrive on Earth and take a series of shuttles to get to Fez, Morocco, where you expect to meet Daemos Titan. As with all old cities, the outer regions are monstrous mega-buildings built in the last few centuries. The inner city is made of skyscrapers built from the 20th to 22nd centuries.`
			`	You turn a corner and see a rare sight for Earth: a large, green, field of grass with a beautiful mosque. It looks like something out of Earth's ancient history, perfectly preserved in present day.`
			choice
				`	(Enter the library.)`
			`	On your way to the library, a scruffy-looking man approaches you. He has a rather large gun in his holster, a massive book strapped to his back, and stares intently at you, or possibly through you. His dark red sunglasses and porcupine beard make it difficult to read or see his face. When the man abruptly stops walking, you consider yelling for help, fearing you're about to be mugged or shot.`
			`	Suddenly, he dives to the nearest bench, pulls the gigantic book off of his back, and starts jotting notes. You're not sure he even noticed you. His mind has wandered to whatever metaphysical world scholars occupy when reality has no bearing on their lives.`
			choice
				`	(Interrupt him.)`
				`	(Watch for a few minutes and see what he does.)`
					goto watch
				`	(Slowly back away from the madman and and ditch the job.)`
					decline
			`	"Excuse me," you say, "can you-"`
			`	Before you can finish your sentence, he waves a hand at you frantically and shouts, "No! No! The southern support column! They always associated South with intellectualism and mystery!"`
				goto question
			label watch
			`	Between bouts of mumbling, reading, ranting, and writing, the man strokes his beard as if petting a cat. Eventually, his frantic scribbling on the giant book runs off the bottom of a page, and his pencil breaks. He is saddened for a moment, but then notices you.`
			`	His beard ruffles as he asks, "Yes?"`
			label question
			`	You ask, "Can you tell me where to find Daemos Titan?"`
			`	He looks baffled. "What would you do if you found him?"`
			`	"I was sent to help him find a book."`
			`	"Right, right. I asked the library for a second Seeker. Are you <last> <first>? Or was it <first> <last>? I'm so bad with names." He begins searching his belongings for another pencil.`
			`	"Captain <last> will do. Are you Daemos Titan?"`
			`	"Yes, yes, yes. I'll get ready to leave. Meet me in the spaceport this evening." He finds his second pencil and shoos you off.`


mission "Alexandria Library Card: First Volume"
	name "Chal Jub Paqmey Search"
	description `Take Daemos Titan to <destination> to search for the first of the five Chal Jub Paqmey.`
	source Earth
	destination Shroud
	passengers 1
	cargo "old books and a thousand pencils" 1
	blocked `Daemos exclaims, "I can't sleep in a gun port! Come back when you have a spare bunk and space for one ton of cargo."`
	to offer
		has "Alexandria Library Card: Offer: done"
	on offer
		conversation
			`When you arrive, you see Daemos Titan, eager to depart. He walks at a brisk pace toward your ship without even telling you his destination. Porters behind push a cart with an impressive number of books and at least a thousand pencils. You notice there is no modern equipment, not even a data pad.`
			`	"Let's go already," he says. "The five Chal Jub Paqmey are not getting any younger."`
			`	"Go where?" you ask.`
			`	"Yes, yes, yes. Details." He fumbles about his person and eventually finds a folded piece of paper in his beard. He shoves it at you so fast that his hand almost hits you. This man hasn't talked to anyone other than himself for so long that he's forgotten basic manners.`
			choice
				`	(Read the paper.)`
			`	The paper is organic, not a modern synthetic, and is stamped with the Alexandria Library Seal using actual wax. The text is badly smudged, but you can make out enough details.`
			`	Your job is to ferry Daemos around the galaxy to find the five Chal Jub Paqmey. You'll receive a payment of 60,000 credits for each book. When you return Daemos and all five Chal Jub Paqmey to Alexandria Station in the Markeb system, you'll receive another 100,000 credits and an Alexandria Library Card.`
			`	The library card will allow permanent access to the Alexandria Station and its library, no matter your legal status, nor the political situation. The library also offers jobs to card holders.`
			choice
				`	"What is a 'Chal Jub Paqmey'?"`
					goto what
				`	"I'm ready to leave whenever you are."`
					goto accept
				`	"Go find another captain. This is all too weird for me."`
					decline
			label what
			`	"'Chal Jub Paqmey' is plural. They're a series of books that record an opera of a merchant-explorer named HoD Qav, wandering a world that seems much bigger than herself. Or was it himself? Anyway, nobody knows the ending since there were supposed to be eight books, but only five were completed. All of them have been lost over the centuries, and I'm going to find them."`
			`	"Where are they?" you ask.`
			`	"If I knew, I wouldn't be searching for them! I just worked out where the first one is, but we'll have to scour space for the other four."`
			choice
				`	"I'm ready to leave whenever you are."`
					goto accept
				`	"Sorry, I don't have time for this."`
					decline
			label accept
			`	"Excellent. Our first destination is <destination>. I suggest you take a route through the western Paradise Planets. We should be pretty safe there."`
				accept
	on visit
		dialog `It seems you left the insane librarian or some of his thousand pencils behind. Perhaps they're in one of your escorts?`
	on complete
		payment 60000
