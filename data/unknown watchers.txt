# You have a 50% chance to collect a new observer every time you land.
# Whenever a new one appears the oldest one "fails" and disappears (if you haven't destroyed it already).
mission "Quarg Observers 1"
	landing
	repeat
	invisible
	to offer
		random < 60
		has "taken quarg technology"
	npc board
		government "Unknown Watchers"
		fleet  "Unknown Watcher"
		dialog "You board one of the drones that's been following you and inspect it, but you are not able to determine its origin. It is made from generic technology and it has been broadcasting on an encrypted frequency.  You have no idea who sent it, but you suspect your acquisition of Quarg technology may have drawn attention from any number of different parties."
	to fail
		# Checking "> 1" means the player will accumulate a max of 2 annoying drones at a time.
		"Quarg Observers 1: active" > 1
	# The destination doesn't matter, since this is invisible and probably never "completed".
	destination Earth
