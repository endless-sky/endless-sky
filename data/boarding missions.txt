mission "Assisting Navy"
	assisting
	repeat
	to offer
		random < 13
	source
		government Republic
	on offer
		conversation
			`After you've helped repair the <origin>, the captain confronts you. "Thank you for helping us back onto our feet, captain <last>. Please accept our gratitude, in the form of <payment>."`
			choice
				`	(Accept payment) "No problem, captain."`
				`	(Refuse payment) "I'm just doing the right thing."`
					goto refuse
			`	"It has been a pleasure meeting you, <last>. May we meet again!"`
				apply
					payment 18000
			label refuse
			`	"If you say so," they seem a little befuddled, but eventually return to their ship and bid farewell.`
				apply
					"reputation: Republic" += 15
