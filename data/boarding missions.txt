mission "Assisting Militia Small"
	assisting
	repeat
		to offer
		random < 75
	source
		government Militia
	destination
		government "Free Worlds"
	on offer
		conversation
			`When you repair the <origin>, the pilot thanks you. "I don't have anything on me, but the Cause owes you, big time. Check in with my unit commander on <destination> and I bet you'll be handsomely rewarded."`
			choice
				`	"Don't worry about it, I'm sure you'd do the same for me."
					decline
				`	"Thanks, I'll look for them."
					accept
	on complete
		conversation
			`News of your deeds has preceded you, and a smiling member of the Militia is waiting for you at the spaceport. "They say you can't repay blood with gold, but I want you to have this token of gratitude for saving one of our own all the same."`
		payment 5000
		decline
